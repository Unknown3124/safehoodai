How Safehood Works

AI-Powered Location

Our AI instantly locates the nearest trained experts and alerts them via GPS when an emergency occurs.

Community Network

Connect with retired nurses, firefighters, and medical professionals who are ready to help in emergencies.

Real-Time Guidance

Step-by-step instructions for CPR, Heimlich maneuvers, and other life-saving procedures for bystanders.

The Problem We Solve

In emergencies, trained professionals and ambulances can face delays in arrival. Bystanders often panic and lack the knowledge to provide immediate assistance, creating a critical gap in emergency response.

Our Solution

Safehood bridges this gap by creating a community-based emergency alert system that instantly connects trained experts with emergencies. Our AI-powered platform ensures that help arrives faster, and our real-time guidance empowers bystanders to take immediate action.

Emergency Medical Guidance

CPR Instructions

1

Check for responsiveness - tap and shout "Are you okay?"

2

Call emergency services (108) immediately

3

Position the person on their back on a firm surface

4

Place the heel of one hand on the center of the chest

5

Place your other hand on top and interlock fingers

6

Push hard and fast - 100-120 compressions per minute

7

Allow chest to recoil completely between compressions

8

Continue until help arrives or person shows signs of life

Heimlich Maneuver

1

Stand behind the person and wrap your arms around their waist

2

Make a fist with one hand and place it above the navel

3

Grasp your fist with your other hand

4

Give quick, upward thrusts into the abdomen

5

Continue until the object is expelled or person becomes unconscious

First Aid Basics

1

Ensure the scene is safe before approaching

2

Check for consciousness and breathing

3

Control bleeding with direct pressure

4

Treat for shock by keeping person warm and comfortable

5

Call emergency services for serious injuries
